#  Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

##  Project Overview

This project presents a **dynamic and interactive Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze business performance across **regions, categories, customers, and time**.

The goal of this dashboard is to enable **data-driven decision-making** by uncovering key sales trends, identifying high-performing areas, and highlighting improvement opportunities.

---

##  Business Objective

Organizations often struggle with:

* Identifying top-performing regions and products
* Understanding seasonal sales trends
* Detecting underperforming segments
* Making quick strategic decisions

This dashboard solves these challenges by providing **real-time, visual insights** in a single view.

---

##  Key Insights 

### 1️ Revenue Performance

* **Total Sales:** 545.23K
* Strong overall performance with consistent contributions across regions
* However, revenue is **not evenly distributed**

   Insight: Business is **region-dependent**, requiring diversification strategy

---

### 2️ Regional Analysis

* **Top Region:** East
* Followed by South and North
* **West is significantly underperforming**

   Business Impact:

* East is the **revenue driver**
* West indicates **market penetration issue or low demand**

   Recommendation:

* Increase marketing or distribution in West
* Analyze customer behavior differences

---

### 3️ Sales Trend (Monthly Pattern)

* Peak sales in **June (~78K)**
* Lowest in **February (~27K)**
* Fluctuating trend throughout the year

   Insight:

* Clear **seasonality pattern**
* Possible external factors: demand cycles, promotions, or inventory

   Recommendation:

* Plan inventory & campaigns before peak months
* Improve sales strategies in low months

---

### 4️ Category Performance

* **Accessories (~56%) dominate sales**
* Electronics contribute less (~44%)

   Insight:

* Accessories are the **primary revenue generator**

   Business Strategy:

* Bundle electronics with accessories
* Upselling opportunity

---

### 5️ Customer-Level Insights

Top customers contribute significantly:

* Krista Morrison (Top customer)
* Anthony Stevens
* Patricia Reid

   Insight:

* Revenue is partially driven by **high-value customers**

   Recommendation:

* Implement **loyalty programs**
* Focus on **customer retention strategy**

---

### 6️ Sales Efficiency Metrics

* **Units Sold:** 592
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 2.73K

   Insight:

* High AOV suggests **premium or bulk purchasing behavior**

   Business Opportunity:

* Introduce targeted discounts to increase volume
* Maintain pricing strategy for high-value customers
